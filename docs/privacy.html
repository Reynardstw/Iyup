Iyup Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Information Used by Iyup

Iyup may request access to your device location to provide location-relevant park, weather, and shade information.

How Information Is Used

Location information is used to provide relevant app functionality and weather information through Apple WeatherKit.

Data Storage

Iyup does not require user registration. The Iyup Team does not operate a backend server that stores users' precise location or personal information.

Your Choices

You can manage location permission through your device's Settings application.
